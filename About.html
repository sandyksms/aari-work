About Us

Our Story

Welcome to Santhosh Aari Works, a brand that celebrates the timeless elegance of Aari embroidery. Our passion for this intricate craft was born out of a desire to blend traditional techniques with modern fashion, creating pieces that are both luxurious and wearable.

Founded in 2021, we have dedicated ourselves to preserving the rich heritage of Aari embroidery while giving it a contemporary twist. Each piece we create is handcrafted by skilled artisans, ensuring the finest quality and detail in every stitch.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to bring the beauty of handcrafted embroidery into the wardrobes of the modern woman. We strive to create pieces that reflect elegance, craftsmanship, and a deep connection to cultural traditions.

Our Craftsmanship

At Santhosh Aari Works, we take pride in the detailed craftsmanship of each piece. Our artisans are highly skilled in the ancient art of Aari embroidery, bringing together tradition and innovation. From vibrant sarees to intricately designed blouses, every item in our collection tells a story of dedication and artistry.
